Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 Feb 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how IronIsh we collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal & Contact Information

We collect the following information when you create or update your profile:

  • Name
  • Email address (via Google Sign-In)
  • Phone number
  • Address details (city, pincode, street address)

This information is provided voluntarily by the user and is used to manage the user profile and improve app functionality.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • GPS or real-time location data
  • Background location
  • Call recordings or call logs

When users open directions, the app redirects to Google Maps via an external link. Any location data used is handled by Google Maps under Google’s Privacy Policy.

3. Phone Call Feature

The app allows users to contact gym/store owners via the device dialer.

  • Calls are not recorded
  • Phone call data is not accessed or stored by the app

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Manage user profiles
  • Enable communication between users and gym/store owners
  • Provide app features and improve user experience

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user personal data with third parties.

Data may be disclosed only if required by law.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information from unauthorized access or misuse.
